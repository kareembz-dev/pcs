About Us

Shaping Tomorrow's Leaders

Pajule Secondary School was established in 2009 as a Church of Uganda (COU) institution, with a clear vision: to provide quality secondary education to the youth of Pajule Town and the wider Pader District in Northern Uganda.

We offer both Ordinary Level (S1–S4) and Advanced Level (S5–S6) programmes, preparing students for Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) and Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) examinations under UNEB.

Our dedicated teachers, faith-based values, and structured academic environment empower every student to achieve their full potential — academically, morally, and spiritually.

Academics

Programmes & Curriculum

We offer a comprehensive curriculum at both O-Level and A-Level, aligned with the Uganda National Curriculum. Students are equipped with knowledge and skills to excel in UNEB examinations and beyond.

Students sit the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) at the end of S4. Core and optional subjects include:

Mathematics

Core subject. Algebra, geometry, statistics and more.

English Language

Core subject. Communication, comprehension and composition.

Biology

Core science. Life processes, ecology and genetics.

Chemistry

Core science. Matter, reactions and organic chemistry.

Physics

Core science. Forces, energy, electricity and waves.

Geography

Physical and human geography of Uganda and the world.

History & Political Education

African history, Ugandan governance and civic education.

Religious Education (CRE)

Christian Religious Education and moral values.

Computer Studies / ICT

Optional. Digital literacy and practical computing.

Agriculture

Optional. Farming, soils, crops and livestock.

Literature in English

Optional. Poetry, drama and prose analysis.

Entrepreneurship

Optional. Business skills and enterprise development.

Fine Art

Optional. Drawing, painting and creative expression.

Kiswahili

Optional. East African language and communication.

Admissions

Join Pajule SS

How to Apply

  • 01

    Visit the School

    Come to our office in Pajule Town to collect an admission form or enquire about available spaces.

  • 02

    Submit Documents

    Bring your PLE results slip (for S1 entry) or O-Level results (for S5 entry), birth certificate and passport photos.

  • 03

    Interview & Placement

    Shortlisted applicants may be called for a brief interview. Students are placed in appropriate classes and streams.

  • 04

    Confirm Enrolment

    Pay the required fees, collect your requirements list, and report on the opening date of the new term.

Entry Requirements

Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) certificate – for S1 entry
UCE / O-Level results slip – for S5 entry
Birth certificate (original + copy)
2 recent passport-size photographs
Transfer certificate (if joining from another school)
Parent/guardian national ID or letter

Contact the school office or visit us during working hours (Mon–Fri, 8:00am – 5:00pm) for all admission enquiries.
